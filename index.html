About Our Institution

Government A.N.M. Training School, Areraj is a Government Nursing Institute committed to excellence in nursing education and rural healthcare services. Our mission is to develop skilled, ethical and compassionate nursing professionals dedicated to community health service.

Principal's Message

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Government A.N.M. Training School, Areraj. Our institution focuses on academic excellence, clinical expertise and humanitarian values to prepare competent nursing professionals for society.
