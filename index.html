About Us

Located near the Subdivisional Hospital in Areraj, East Champaran, the ANM Training School is dedicated to training, nurturing, and educating young women to become skilled Auxiliary Nurse Midwives.


Our focus is on providing comprehensive training in maternal health, child health, and community health services to meet the healthcare needs of rural communities.
